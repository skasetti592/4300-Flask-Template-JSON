GRUB CITY

To use this app, please input in your desired price, location, and meal time. This app will help you find the ideal restaurant to visit according to your specifictation!
For more context, you will be given a similarity score and a description that explains why that result was matched.
Then, you can use the heart button to "like" the places you want to visit and view them using the "View Liked" button.
To find similar restaurants to the ones you liked, you can press the "Update Now" button.
